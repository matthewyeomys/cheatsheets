\paragraph{Invariants}
\textcolor{Blue}{Bubble}: Largest $j$ items sorted in the final $j$ positions
after the $j$-th iteration
Bubble sort is stable.\\
\textcolor{Blue}{Selection}: Smallest $j$ items sorted in the first $j$
positions after the $j$-th iteration
Selection sort is non-stable.\\
\textcolor{Blue}{Insertion}: First $j$ positions are sorted after the $j$-th
iteration
Insertion sort is stable.\\
\textcolor{Blue}{Merge}: $tmp[j]$ is the minimum of all remaining elements
after the $j$-th iteration
Mergesort is stable.\\
\textcolor{Blue}{Quick}: B is partitioned around pivot\\
\verb|A[high]>pivot| at the end of each iter
\begin{verbatim}
void quickSort(arr, n)
   if (n == 1) then return;
   else
      p = partition(arr[1..n], n)
      quickSort(arr[1..p-1], p-1)
      quickSort(arr[p+1..n], n-p)
\end{verbatim}
Quicksort [$O(n\log n)$ version] is non-stable.\\
